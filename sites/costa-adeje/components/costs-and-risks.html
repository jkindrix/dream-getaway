Risk & Responsibility FAQ

What if someone bails?

  • Escrow fund covers short-term gap

  • 30-day grace + penalties

  • Persistent default = automatic dilution or buyout

What if someone hogs the property?

  • Credit-based booking calendar with rollover points

  • Everyone gets fair access in high/low seasons

What if big repairs come up?

  • Funded from annual reserve

  • $5K = group vote (75% approval)

What if someone wants to sell?

  • First right of refusal at appraised market value

  • If declined, open to external vetted buyer

What if we just can't agree?

  • Majority vote settles normal disputes

  • Deadlock = third-party mediation → arbitration clause

This structure keeps things fair, fun, and friction-free.

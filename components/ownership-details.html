Why This Makes Sense for Us

  • Shared cost = luxury that's financially reachable

  • Shared calendar = fair use, guaranteed time for each of us

  • Shared responsibility = light load on maintenance, taxes, and decisions

  • Full equity ownership = no gimmicks, no timeshare, real property

  • Optional rental revenue = offset ownership costs if desired

It's not just affordable—it's strategically smart co-ownership.

Ownership Structure & Governance

Legal Entity

  • Form a U.S.-based LLC or Spanish Sociedad Civil

  • Each person owns 25% equity through the entity

Operating Agreement Provisions

  • Buy/sell clauses (with right of first refusal)

  • Usage schedule with calendar fairness and rollover credits

  • Expense sharing: taxes, maintenance, reserve funds

  • Voting rights for major decisions (75% approval threshold)

  • Emergency fund and escrow for missed payments

  • Default clauses: late penalties, dilution risk, force-out procedures

Exit & Conflict Resolution

  • If one wants out: others have priority to buy at market value

  • Conflict deadlock: escalate to mediator → binding arbitration

  • Optional property manager to handle local affairs

Legal & Tax

  • Hire Spanish notary and English-speaking attorney

  • Appoint a local accountant for tax filings and compliance

This ensures total clarity, fairness, and enforceability for everyone involved.
